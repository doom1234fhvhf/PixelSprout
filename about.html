About PixelSprout

A student-built creative lab developed for the Technology Student Association Web Design competition.

PixelSprout was created to demonstrate real-world web development through intentional design, organized code, and interactive features.

Every aspect of this website—including layout, branding, animations, and JavaScript functionality—was designed and developed during the current school year by students, without templates or frameworks.

Originality

All content, visuals, and code were created entirely by students.

Accessibility

Semantic HTML, readable contrast, and keyboard-friendly navigation.

Professionalism

Industry-style structure, modular CSS, and development best practices.
