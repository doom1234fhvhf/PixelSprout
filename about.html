About PixelSprout

PixelSprout is a fictional creative lab designed and developed by students for the Technology Student Association Web Design competition.

The goal of PixelSprout is to demonstrate real-world web development practices through clean design, organized code, and interactive features. No templates, frameworks, or third-party libraries were used.

Every aspect of this site—including layout, branding, imagery, animations, and JavaScript functionality—was created during the current school year.

Originality

All content and code are student-created.

Accessibility

Keyboard navigation and readable contrast are prioritized.

Professionalism

Industry-style structure and best practices.
